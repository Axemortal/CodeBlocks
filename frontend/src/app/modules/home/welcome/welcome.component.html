Welcome to CodeBlocks!
How to play:
1. Listen to your teacher's instructions
2. Assemble your blocks into the order you want your robot to move in
3. Ask your teacher to scan your blocks
4. Check your scan with your teacher
5. Press "Run Code"
6. Watch your robot go!
